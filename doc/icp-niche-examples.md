### Track for Each Sub-ICP:
1. **Cost Per Click (CPC)** - Benchmark: $1-3 for B2B
2. **Landing Page Conversion Rate** - Benchmark: 5-15% to waitlist
3. **Cost Per Signup (CPL)** - Benchmark: $10-30
4. **Prepayment Conversion Rate** - Benchmark: 10-30% of signups
5. **Cost Per Prepayment** - Benchmark: $30-100

### Decision Criteria:
- **Kill if:** CPL > $50 after 100+ clicks
- **Double down if:** Prepayment conversion > 20% and CPL < $30
- **Test more if:** Good CPL but low prepayment (may need copy/offer adjustment)
